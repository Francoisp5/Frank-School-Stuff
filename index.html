Recipe #1 Pork Ribs

  • Time: 12-24H
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Ketchup 1 cup
Molasses 1/4 cup
Vinegar 1/4 cup
Salt 1/2 tsp
Pepper 1/8 tsp
Onion Powder 1/8 tsp
Onion salt 1/8 tsp
Brown sugar 3 tbsp
Chives 2 tbsp
Pork ribs 3 pound

Step 1:Boil ribs in water with salt, pepper, a medium onion cut into six and 6-7 drops of liquid smoke for 1 hour. Drain and let cool.

Step 2:Mix all the marinade ingredients. Put the ribs in a baggie and coat the ribs marinade, marinate overnight and cook for dinner.

Step 3:Place the ribs on a rack in a roasting pan and bake at 325°F for 3 hours, basting halway through and at the end of cooking. They can also be cook on the grill, turning halfway through cooking.

Recipe #2 Ailes de poulet du Cactus

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
HP57 sauce 1 cup
Frank's red hot sauce 1/2 cup
Butter 1/3 cup
Brown sugar 1/3 cup
Pepper 1/8 tsp
Chiken wings 24-30

Step 1: Mix all ingredients and stir at low for 15 min

Step 2: Put wings in the fryer at 190°C for 15 min.

Step 3: Put wing and the sauce into a large bowl and stir.

Recipe #3 Marinade au Jack Daniel's

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Jack Daniel's Whisky 1/4 cup
Soy Sauce 1/4 cup
Dijon Mustard 1/4 cup
Scalions 1/4 cup
Brown Sugar 1/4 cup
Salt 1 tsp
English Sauce 1 tsp

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in a baggie

Step 2: Put beef, chiken or pork in the baggie and let it marinate overnight
